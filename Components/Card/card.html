Card

A card is a group of related information, generally comprising of an Image, headers and footers, a wide variety of content, and sometimes also has a call to action button. In order to only use the Button Component from Cascade UI in your project, copy and paste the below code in the head tag of your html document. 

                
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cascadeUI.netlify.app/css/foundation.css" />
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cascadeUI.netlify.app/Components/Image/image.css" />
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cascadeUI.netlify.app/Components/Button/button.css" />
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cascadeUI.netlify.app/Components/Card/card.css" />
